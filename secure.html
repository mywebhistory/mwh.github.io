Privacy

We want to make sure that we can not access your browser’s history and we take all possible measures to make you in control of your data.

  • You data can not be decrypted without the master password
  • We do not store the master password used for encrypting your browser’s history
  • Only you can access the history data with you master password

Zero knowledge .. almost

As much as we love you, we do not want to know about you. However, for operational purposes we need to know a few details. We want to be upfront about it. So here is what we know (or don't):

History data No. We do not have access to it
Email Yes. That's how we identify you
Browser, IP Yes. We have this data for debugging

Encryption

We use proven encrption to protect your data

  • We use SHA-512 for securing your login password
  • We use AES-256-GCM for master password
  • The connection between your browser and our server is over HTTPS
