REFUND PROCESS

Overview

Thanks for considering our subscription at MyWebHisotry.com operated by MyWebHistory. We understand that you could change your mind about mywebhistory products at any time. If you are not satisfied with the product that you have purchased from us, you can get your money back no questions asked. You are eligible for a full reimbursement (excluding the taxes) within 7 calendar days of your purchase. After the 7-day period you will no longer be eligible and won't be able to receive a refund. We encourage our customers to try the service in the first two weeks after their purchase to ensure it fits your needs. If you have any additional questions or would like to request a refund, feel free to contact us at payments@mywebhistory.com.



Refunds (if applicable)

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If your refund request is approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within a certain amount of days.



Late or missing refunds (if applicable)

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at payments@mywebhistory.com.



Cancellation

We understand that at times you may want to cancel the subscription to the service. While we will be sad to lose you as a customer and a friend, we would also want to make the cancellation process simple and easy. To cancel your subscription, please follow the following steps:

  • Login to your mywebhistory.com account
  • Go to manage your account
  • From select the subscription and click cancel
  • Confirm that you want to cancel
  • You will receive a confirmation email from us about your cancellation

Do keep in mind that:
  • To avoid getting charged for the next billing period, you need to cancel at least one day before the billing date
  • Cancellation will end billing and delete all your data. We will not be able to recover this data for you.


For all queries, feel free to write to us at payments@mywebhistory.com.
