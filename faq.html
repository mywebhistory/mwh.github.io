Get Started

  • Why is it not free?
  • Running the service and infrastructure has costs. We do not want to reap that cost by selling your data. Remember, if you are not paying for the product, you are the product.
  • Will my data be shared with advertisers?
  • No. We recognize that it's very tempting. To keep us straight and narrow, we encrypt all of your data with a key that's only known to you. We do not have the capability to decrypt your data without your key.
  • What browsers do you support?
  • We support Mozilla and Chrome at this point. We are planning to support Microsoft Edge in near future.
  • Can you delete my data from your servers?
  • Yes. You can request a purge anytime (after login) from your dashboard.

  • How do you secure my data?
  • We secure your data by leveraging encryption as much as possible. We do decryption only in your browser, so that your data is never decrypted on our servers.
  • I have lost the key, can you recover my data?

  • Ouch!! unfortunately no. Because your data is encrypted with a unique key, that only you have, we can not recover data without that key. May we suggest that you use a password manager in future to simplify your password management.

  • Can MWH be bypassed?
  • Yes. Like most of the softwares, this can be bypassed. However, we provide you instructions in the support section to minimize that surface.
  • Where can I find information about installation and usage?
  • You can visit support.mywebhisotry.com to see all information pertaining to installation and dashboard usage.
  • I need help. Who do I reach out to?
  • We provide email support for the product. Please send an email to support@mywebhistory.com and remember to include the following details:
    • Description of the problem you are facing
    • Details of the browser where MWH is installed

  • Can I get a bulk discount?
  • We can talk. Please send an email to support@mywebhistory.com.
  • I do not want to make a commitment. What payment method should I choose?
  • If you do not want to subscribe, you can still keep paying for monthly service and use it. You will receive an email invoice every month with payment link.
  • What if I miss a payment?
  • We wil try our best to remind you of the upcoming due dates. In case you miss the payment, we will keep your account active (including data) for a grace period of 7 days. If the payment is not received within the grace period, we will deactivate your account. You data will be purged from our system 3 weeks after that.
  • What payment methods do you support?
  • For one time payment, we support UPI, netbanking and cards. For monthly subscription, we support cards & netbanking.
  • Is the price includes taxes?
  • Yes, the advertised price is inclusive of all taxes. What you see is what you pay.
  • Can I cancel my subscription?
  • Yes, you can. We initiate a refund to you in 305 business days. Do keep in mind that refund will be only for the service fee. We can not refund taxes (Govt taketh and not giveth). Please check our refund policy for more details.
