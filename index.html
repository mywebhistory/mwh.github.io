Private & secure storage of browser history

Browser history is private and we respect your privacy. We use end-to-end encryption to ensure that only you have access to your history data. Without your key, even we can not access to your browsing history.

Learn more

Intuitive dashboard for easy review

You can login to your account and review your browser history at anytime, from anywhere. We give you different views of your data to help you analyze how your devices are being used for online activities. Deleting history from the browser will not delete data from your cloud account.

Learn More

Retain data upto 12 months

You can retain your history data for upto one year, based on the plan that you choose. We give you easy search options for searching through your data in your dashboard.

Learn More

Zero knowledge encryption

We understand how tempting it can be to look into the data we have. To keep the temptation at bay, we only trust you with encryption key. All the browser history data we have for you, is encrypted right in your browser with a key that only you have knowledge of. This data is only ever decrypted in your browser, after your enter the key. Hence we are confident you remain in control of your security.
